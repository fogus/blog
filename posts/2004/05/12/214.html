I used to play a game with my friends where they would give me a random phrase, and I would draw a picture to it (Rob has played this game in the past). I often wondered what would occur if a group of people were given a seemingly meaningless phrase as the title of an unwritten picture. I would imagine that the range of ideas would be interesting; as long as the phrase was not apt to conjure stereotypical images. It might be fun to try one day.
