Doctor Who?

Doctor Who is making a comeback on BBC television and the kind folks over at Gallfrey One have been stalking the sets and snapping photos of the shoots. The newest incarnation of the Doctor will be played by Christopher Eccleston from 28 Days Later (the mad military leader) and The Others (the husband) fame. I am pretty psyched about the new series having almost come full circle with the past episodes (I’m slowly working my way through the 1st Doctor, and then should be done). Doctor Who holds a special place in my heart as it was the only interesting show on (besides Red Dwarf) at 2am. During high school I would often sneak out of the house with my friends to SK8, and was able to catch the episodes when I returned. When did I sleep you ask? In class of course. Anyway, after I graduated I decided not to go directly to college, but instead chose to try and make my way in the real world. As it turns out, the real world sucks and I quickly learned that the lala land that is college was the place for me. In retrospect, the only real advantage of taking that year off was that I managed to see the entire run of Tom Baker episodes during that time. Everything else was a wash.
